About Us

This group serves as the Austin chapter of PostgreSQL user groups, a community supported gathering of postgres users new and experienced alike.

PGDay Austin 2017 will be held on August 26th in Austin, TX at fibercove, a coworking, meeting, and event space in the South Lamar district of Austin, TX. This event is hosted by the Austin PostgreSQL User Group (PUG), and underwritten by the U.S. PostgreSQL Association. The Austin PUG serves as the Austin chapter of PostgreSQL user groups, a community supported gathering of Postgres users new and experienced alike.
