PgDay Austin 2017

- Don't Mess with Postgres -

PGDay Austin 2017 will be held on August 27th in Austin, TX at fibercove, a coworking, meeting, and event space in the South Lamar district of Austin, TX. This group serves as the Austin chapter of PostgreSQL user groups, a community supported gathering of Postgres users new and experienced alike.
